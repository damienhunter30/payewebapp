PAYE Calculator

If you want to support my work, as I am unemployed and attending college, I would love any help at all and you can donate if you'd like. I've developed this application for PAYE workers in Ireland. The application should provide a number of options for a user to choose from, depending on each individuals situation.

The input is the users annual salary before any tax is deducted. As a user, you should choose any option that applies to you. The output you'll see towards the bottom of the page, will be your net salary. One thing to consider. Your calculated net salary is an estimation of what you can expect to be paid.
